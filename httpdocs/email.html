IdeaBase Email Signature

What To Do:
  • Using Google Chrome only, highlight and Copy the entire signature template (including the logo!) below.
  • Paste the email signature into your email client's signature settings.
  • Modify the Name, Title, Phone Number and Email Address
  • Don't forget to click on the email address and update the email address link!
Gmail Web Interface Notes:
  • Edit Signatures under Settings > General > Signature.
  • You can edit your email address by clicking once on the email address and going to Change
Apple Mail Notes:
  • Edit Signatures by going to Mail > Preferences > Signatures and set a signature for your IdeaBase email. Set it to always show under Choose Signature.
  • You won't see the logo when you first paste, but it will appear when you write your first email.
  • Edit the link by Right clicking and going to Link > Edit Link
  • Apple Mail will change the color of your email address when you update it, but you can highlight the email address, right click on the email address, go to Font > Show Colors and change the Hex Color under the 2nd tab to 00a8e1 if you want.
